Banana bread


Banana bread is a type of sweet bread or cake made from mashed bananas. It is often a moist and sweet quick bread but some recipes are yeast raised.


Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking soda
  • pinch of salt
  • Butter
  • Brown sugar
  • Eggs
  • Bananas

Steps

  1. Combine the dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Beat the butter and sugar in another bowl. Add eggs and bananas
  3. Add the wet mixture to the dry mixture
  4. Pour the batter into a loaf pan and bake.
